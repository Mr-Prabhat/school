About Us

Our Mission

To provide quality education that empowers students to become responsible citizens and lifelong learners, equipped with the knowledge and skills to succeed in an ever-changing world.

Our Vision

To be a leading educational institution in Lumbini Province, recognized for academic excellence, character development, and community engagement.

Our History

Established in 1995, Prabhat Higher Secondary School has been serving the educational needs of the community for over 25 years. What began as a small school with just a handful of students has grown into a premier institution with modern facilities and a reputation for excellence.

Our Values

  • Academic Excellence
  • Inclusive Education
  • Character Development
  • Community Engagement
  • Innovation in Teaching
